About me

I am an undergraduate data science student and researcher at the University of Iowa (expected December, 2024). I am excited by the possibilities of using computer science and statistics to solve big problems confronting us in the 21st century. I am particularly interested in climate change, ecosystem health, and public health.

Historically, data collection for scientists was slow, limited and expensive. Now, our data collection and storage capabilities have skyrocketed and many scientific fields are no longer limited by lack of data. A new limiting factor can be data science -- having sophisticated enough statistical knowledge and coding skills to meaningfully interpret large datasets.

As data availability continues to grow, I hope to continue to grow my skills to match.
